About

London Color Palette is a color palette built from famous London landmarks.

6 London Landmarks, 6 UI Colors

That experience sparked an idea of a color palette based on pictures London landmarks. I had previously frequented popular color palettes like Flat UI Colors and Material UI Colors, but this London-based palette would be different. It would have extra, invaluable features like information on color-psychology, and darker and lighter versions of colors built-in. Most of all, London Color Palette is based around famous and beautiful landmarks in this great city, and those beautiful landmarks will sure make for a beautiful UI for your needs.

For the colors, I personally went out in London to famous landmarks, searching for the perfect colors for this palette, taking pictures along the way.

This site is open-sourced on GitHub, and you can check out my website, xtrp.io for my blog, projects, and more. I really hope you enjoy this site and its features, and thank you so much for visiting it.

— Fred Adams, December 6, 2019
